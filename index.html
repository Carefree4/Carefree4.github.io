MemTrack

I created a project for FBLA, a high school compaction for high school students interested in business. The event I competed in was Desktop Application development. In Desktop Application Development, each person was assigned a client with a business problem, we then made an application to solve our client's problem and presented it to them. The application I was asked to build was to keep track of members for the FBLA organization. I was awarded first place in the West Central Region.


Key features of the application include:

  • Emailing reports from the app
  • XML manipulation
  • Importing and exporting Excel files from the app
  • Data validation
  • Date: 2015
  • Service: Software Development, Business Automation

Price Check

-Photo by Michael Righi

With major online retail stores, the merchant with the lowest price wins the front page for that product. This client was having trouble making sure his many products on this online store always was the first listed.

I created a small application that took an excel file of all of his UPC codes and their respective prices and notified the client if any of their prices were not the most competitive. Allowing the client to guarantee their products showed up first on this online store.

  • Date: March 2017
  • Service: Business Automation
